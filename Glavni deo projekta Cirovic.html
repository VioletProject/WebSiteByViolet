GOOGLE APPS

Gmail

Gmail is a free Web e-mail service, which is owned by Google. Gmail was first used in April 2004, and every user has used 1GB of storage space. The interface is based on Ajax technology and JavaScript technologies. Users are able to use all of Google's algorithms and all techniques for using e-mail. Gmail is available in forty languages and today we can say that Gmail interface can runs on almost every version of every browser. The offered storage space is constantly increasing speed 4 bytes per one second. The available storage space per user amounted to more than 4GB on 22 October 2007. Google is first enabled users to talk directly with the web interface using Ajax, but later made GTalk application for users who want to chat via the web interface. There are also some criticism of Gmail, but the biggest criticism is related to targeted advertising, which disturb the privacy of the user and it was a big problem for many users. Nonetheless, we can say that Gmail is very useful for users who want to exchange e-mail and because of that Gmail is very popular.

Google Drive

Google Drive is a service that is used for storage, but it also serves to synchronize the files. We can say that Google Drive allows its users to share and store files in the cloud. This disc includes Google Docs, Sheets, and office suite that called Slides. Google Drive has had 240 million monthly active users by October 2014. The client communicates with Google Drive and synchronize data, but Google Drive client's software must be installed on the user's computer. Google Drive can be accessed offline by using the Google Chrome Chrome apps that can be installed from the Chrome Web Store. Documents, spreadsheets, presentations and drawings can be seen and be modified offline via independent Chrome applications. Google Drive for Android and iOS supports offline view all file types. Offline editing of various documents and also tables are supported through different standalone applications for mobile devices. Google offers an extension for Google Chrome Save to Google Drive, which allows many users to store Web content on Google Drive through action or search through the menu.

Google Docs, Sheets, Slides and Forms

Google Docs
Google Docs allows the creation of all documents similar to the Office package, but with limited options since they are still accessed via the web interface. Google Docs are enough tool for simpler documents.
Google Sheets
Google Sheets makes your data pop with colorful charts and graphs. This is a useful and very good way to effectively stylize your data and present them graphically. On the Internet you can find good examples.
Google Slides
Google Slides is an online presentations app that allows you to show off your works in a visual ways. You can do different things like creating and editing presentations, editing a presentation and sharing it effortlessly.
Google Forms
With Google Forms, you can create surveys right in your web browser. Google Forms provide a fast way to create an online survey for people, with different responses collected in an online available spreadsheet.

Google Sites

Google Sites is a free service designed to establish communication between users. Users can connect to each other and also to cooperate with each other. This free service is performed in an surroundings that is specifically designed to provide users with efficient communication. Google Sites started his project in a manner similar to JotSpot and allowed him the good progress of the initiated process. JotSpot is a product name of a software company that is dedicated to small and medium-sized enterprises and this company was founded by Joe Kraus and also Graham Spencer. Each page that was created using Google Page Creator moved on Google Sites in 2007, and Google Sites was unveiled using the JotSpot technology the following year. This service was free, but the use of the domain cost ten dollars. Finally, Google Sites became totally free without the need for domain on 21 May 2008 and it was a positive turning point for the users of these services. It is useful to say that all users who choose to use Google Sites must respect regulations that prohibits the abuse of this service. This service may be useful if the user respects the policy.

Google Calendar

Google Calendar allows users to create and edit events. It is available as a web application and as mobile apps for the Android and also iOS mobile operating systems. Using Google Calendar, you can add many events and send invitations effortlessly, share with friends and family (or keep for yourself), and search the web looking for the events in which you will enjoy. Google Calendar represents simplified the organization. Positive facts relating to Google Calendar as free use of this application, the availability of interfaces in many languages and completely supporting browsers Microsoft IE, Mozilla Firefox and Safari browser. We have to say that in all browsers must be enabled JavaScript and cookies. Google Calendar settings are organized into foursections: General settings, calendars, Setting up mobile devices and laboratory settings (labs). Google calendar is a useful application that can do a lot to facilitate the organization of daily activities.

Google Hangouts

Google Hangouts is a communication platform developed by Google which includes instant messaging, video chat, SMS and VOIP features. Hangouts is synced automatically across devices, so you can start a Hangout on your computer and set it to another device. You can start a Hangout with one person or to participate in a group chat with several people. Calls on almost all the numbers in the US and Canada are free from all countries where calling is available through Hangouts. Calls to several destinations in the US and Canada will cost 1 cent (USD) per minute or the listed rate for your local currency. You can send text messages using Hangouts via Google Voice Project Fi or phone number. On Android devices, you can send text messages using Hangouts across a number of mobile operators. You can share photos and stickers in conversations. On mobile devices, you can also share your location with other people. You should know that it is necessary to have a microphone and a camera that you can use video calling in this application. Utilization of Hangouts is a good way to establish communication with people who are far away.

Google+

Google+ or Google plus is a interest-based social web network owned by Google. Assessments of Google plus growth have varied widely because Google first defined the service as a social network, then later as a social layer across all of Google's services allowing them to sharing a user's identity and their interests. When we talk about the technology that used by Google+ we can say that it uses Java servlets for the server code and JavaScript for the browser-side of the UI. Google announced the Google+ innovative improvements: automatic hideaway and poor quality comments, zooming some photos on Google+ web network, restoring the "Events" item and machine learning which will adjust certain size of the images. In July 2014, Google plus policy was changed to allow any name to be used.

Jamboard

Jamboard is a product made by Google, which is a digital panel that aims to quickly share ideas between team members. Utilization of Jamboard encourages progress of creativity in whole team, and therefore the number of creative ideas increases quickly in a short period of time. The result of that is an increase in productivity and efficiency in the work of the entire team. Jamboard has many advantages that are more advanced than usual old table. First of all, this digital whiteboard that we call Jamboard allows us to automatically record all our ideas that we presented at the meeting. Then, we are able to share our ideas mentioned with all the members of our team using digital properties of this digital helpful table. Jamboard is intelligently designed to speed up collaboration among your team and it consists of a built-in HD camera and speakers. Jamboard’s touchscreen was built for precision drawing and it automatically to recognize the difference between using the stylus to sketch or the eraser to start over. In 2017 you can purchase Jamboard with affordable price that is more favorable price than other touchscreen displays. This is a great advantage and a great opportunity to improve the creativity of your business.
